# IGL Potable Spirits Redesign: Website Structure & Copy

This document details the final layout structure, visual styling guidelines, and complete copywriting used for the **IGL Potable Spirits** landing page.

---

## 🎨 Creative & Visual System

*   **Theme:** "Raising the Bar for India’s Premium Pour" — blending corporate listing weight with premium spirits warmth.
*   **Palette:**
    *   **Background:** Deep Obsidian (`#070709`) and Charcoal (`#0c0c0f`).
    *   **Accents:** Liquid Amber and Brushed Gold (`#D4AF37`).
    *   **Text:** Platinum White (`#f4f4f7`) and Warm Muted Silver (`#a0a0aa`).
*   **Typography:**
    *   **Headings:** *Bebas Neue* (All-caps Condensed Display).
    *   **Body & Stats:** *Roboto* (Clean Sans-Serif).

---

## 📂 Section-by-Section Overview

### 1. Navigation Header
*   **Elements:** Dynamic blurred sticky container with SVG Logo and updated links: `Our Distillery`, `The Process`, `Our Story`, `Articles`, and `Contact`.

### 2. Hero Section
*   **Headline:** *Raising the Bar for India’s Premium Pour*
*   **Subheadline:** *Backed by the scale of a publicly listed manufacturing powerhouse. Driven by the momentum of a fast-growing spirits portfolio across whisky, vodka, rum, and volume-leading categories.*
*   **Actions:** Solid gold button `Explore Portfolio` and outline button `Financial Highlights`.
*   **Visual:** Silhouette render of the *Single Reserve Whisky* bottle glowing over an amber background aura.

### 3. Investor Snapshot (Financial Statistics Grid)
*   **Interactive Counter Metrics:**
    *   **₹1,331 Cr** — *FY26 Revenue* (Growing at +14.4% YoY).
    *   **₹285 Cr** — *FY26 EBIT* (Growing at +11.1% YoY).
    *   **23.7 Million** — *Cases Sold in 9MFY26* (Broad national presence).
    *   **53%** — *EBIT Contribution Mix* (Highest contributing segment to consolidated profits).

### 4. The Premiumisation Curve (Volume to Value Switcher)
*   **Toggle Options:** `Volume Engine` (Stable cash flows) vs `Value Driver` (High margins).
*   **Volume Copy:** Focuses on Bunty Bubbly (14M+ cases sold, India Book of Records holder) and IGL's dominance in UP & Uttarakhand (over 50% country liquor market share in Uttarakhand).
*   **Value Copy:** Details IGL's pivot towards premium IMFL brands (Soulmate Blu, Zumba Rums, Amazing Vodka, Amrut Partnership) yielding a high **21.2% EBIT margin in 9MFY26**.

### 5. Curated Portfolio (Tabbed Brand Carousel)
*   **Tabs:**
    *   **Whisky:** *Single Reserve Whisky* (traditional cask aged) and *Soulmate Blu* (millionaire-case seller).
    *   **Vodka:** *Amazing Vodka* (five-times filtered, imported German flavours) and Fruit Flavours (Apple, Orange, Cranberry).
    *   **Rum:** *Zumba Lemoni White Rum* (citrus white rum) and *Zumba Black Spiced Rum*.
    *   **Economy & Country:** *Bunty Bubbly* (14M+ cases volume leader) and *Dhurandhar*.
    *   **Partner Brands:** *Amrut's MaQintosh Special Edition Whisky* (Grand Gold Winner) and *Old Port Rum*.

### 6. Industrial Backbone (Manufacturing & Infrastructure)
*   **Cards:**
    *   **Dual Site Infrastructure:** Kashipur and Gorakhpur distilleries producing premium ENA.
    *   **Self-Sufficient Supply Chain:** Captive ENA, in-house bottling lines, and automated packaging.
    *   **Global Co-Packing Alliance:** Over 10 years of packing, blending, and maturation operations for Bacardi.

### 7. Legacy Timeline (1999 - 2026)
*   **Interactive Milestones:**
    *   **1999:** Kashipur continuous distillery commissioned.
    *   **2002:** Bottling integration and entry into domestic alcobev.
    *   **2006:** Gorakhpur distillery commissioned.
    *   **2010:** Bacardi strategic alliance launched.
    *   **2021:** Premium IMFL launches (Single Reserve, Amazing Vodka).
    *   **2024:** Amrut Distilleries portfolio partnership.
    *   **2026:** Record ₹1,331 Cr revenue performance.

### 8. Complete Portfolio (3D Flip Grid)
*   **Interaction:** Inspired by Cherrapunji's botanicals section, featuring a 6-card responsive grid layout. On hover, cards flip in 3D to reveal brand information:
    *   **Card 1:** Single Reserve Whisky (Oak-Cask aged)
    *   **Card 2:** Amazing Vodka (Five-Times Filtered)
    *   **Card 3:** Zumba Spiced Rum (Tropical Spiced)
    *   **Card 4:** Amrut MaQintosh (Premium Blend)
    *   **Card 5:** Bunty Special Whisky (High-Volume Value)
    *   **Card 6:** Bunty Bubbly (Market Leader)
*   **Visual States:** Front side displays high-resolution bottle renders or thematic imagery; back side flips to display premium details and dynamic CTA triggers.
