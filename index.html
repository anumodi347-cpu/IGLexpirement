Premium Whisky

Heritage & Craftsmanship

IGL's award-winning whiskeys balance rich oak-aged traditional malt profiles with a modern, contemporary finish. Led by Soulmate Blu, a millionaire-case brand that holds a leadership position in Delhi and Uttarakhand, and Single Reserve, aged in oak casks, we deliver sophistication in every sip.

Award-Winning Vodka

Amazing Vodka Plain & Flavours

Five-times filtered and blended with extra-neutral alcohol (ENA) alongside premium natural flavours imported from Germany. Amazing Vodka commands double-digit market share in major launch markets and has earned multiple Gold and Silver Spiritz Selection Awards for tasting and packaging.

Premium Rum

Zumba Lemoni & Spiced Rum

Experience the tropical rhythm of premium spirits. Zumba Lemoni White Rum features a vibrant citrus profile with a double-digit market share in key retail territories, while Zumba Black Spiced Rum brings a rich blend of natural molasses and spices.

The Industrial Backbone

We control every step of the supply chain, ensuring unmatched ENA purity, quality compliance, and manufacturing scalability.

Dual Distillery Infrastructure

Our plants at Kashipur and Gorakhpur feature high-capacity grain-based and molasses continuous distillation systems, producing exceptionally pure Extra Neutral Alcohol (ENA).

Self-Sufficient Supply Chain

Captive ENA production, on-site glass & carton packaging plants, and high-efficiency automated bottling lines reduce costs and protect against supply disruptions.

Global Co-Packing Alliance

For more than 10 years, IGL has been a trusted business partner for Bacardi, managing blending, regulatory approvals, and maturation plants for their international portfolios.

A Legacy of Value Creation

How IGL evolved from a core manufacturing distillery in 1999 to a premium multi-brand alcobev force in FY26.

1999

Distillery Commissioning

Kashipur distillery commissioned with an 85,000 BL per day continuous process. Started supplying high-quality ENA.

2002

Bottling & IMFL Launch

Established direct bottling integration. Commenced the domestic country liquor and economy alcobev business.

2006

Gorakhpur Distillery

Commissioned the Gorakhpur facility, doubling our distillation footprint and expanding distribution capacity across northern states.

2010

The Bacardi Alliance

Initiated our co-packing alliance with Bacardi, handling localized production, maturation, and regulatory compliance.

2021

Premium IMFL Launch

Introduced our proprietary premium segment: Single Reserve Whisky and Amazing Vodka plain & flavored lines.

2024

Amrut Distilleries Alliance

Signed a strategic co-branding and production alliance with Amrut. Launched MaQintosh Special Edition and Old Port Rum.

2026

₹1,331 Cr Revenue Landmark

Potable spirits records ₹1,331 Cr revenue and ₹285 Cr EBIT, representing the single highest year of premium growth and profitability.
